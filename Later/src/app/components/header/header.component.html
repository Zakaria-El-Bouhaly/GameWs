Chtara
  • The first player creates a game a PIN is generated and the second player joins the game using the PIN.
  • The first player starts the game by clicking on the start button.
  • The game starts with 20 boxes each box contains a random amount of money between 1 and 1 000 000
  • The first player starts by selecting a box and keeps it until the end of the game, and then he selects a box and the amount of money in the box is revealed.
  • after 8 selections the first player will wait for the second player to make an offer to buy the intially selected box, or ignore.
  • the first player can accept or reject the offer.
  • The game is over when the first player selects the last box. or by accepting an offer from the second player.
  • the winner is the player with the highest amount of money
